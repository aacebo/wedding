## Location

San Pancrazio, 7 km from Lucca, ~40 min from Pisa Airport. Province of Lucca, Tuscany.

## Why it ranks here

This jumps way up now that trees and beauty matter more than pool centrality. It has major villa character and strong tree-filled grounds with a 9-hectare botanical park.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€65k–€95k**

## Pros

* Gorgeous formal villa with period furnishings, frescoed rooms, and Indian/Chinese-decorated salons
* 9-hectare botanical park with English Garden, Italian Garden, 17th-century Limonaia, and open-air theatre (Teatro di Verzura)
* Multiple pools — 3 swimming pools across the estate, plus an 18m infinity pool at L'Arancera (sister property)
* Strong onsite accommodation — four independent rental houses plus a B&B; L'Arancera alone sleeps 24–32 guests in 12 bedrooms
* Capacity for 300+ guests
* Excellent for photos and overall ambience

## Cons

* Pricing is less transparent
* Located near Lucca rather than Florence (~1.5 hours from Florence)
* Slightly less straightforward than a simpler buyout estate

## Verdict

Probably the best beauty + trees + practicality hybrid in the list. Accommodation and pool situation are both stronger than initially thought.
