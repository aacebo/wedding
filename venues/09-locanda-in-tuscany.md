## Location

Castiglione d'Orcia, Province of Siena, Tuscany. In the UNESCO World Heritage Val d'Orcia.

## Why it ranks here

A beautifully restored 17th-century private mansion with sweeping Val d'Orcia views. Previously ranked higher when pool was weighted more heavily — the 20m pool is a genuine standout. Drops under the new brief because pure estate beauty and tree cover matter more.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€50k–€80k**

## Pros

* 20m pool with stunning Val d'Orcia valley views — excellent for the jump-in
* UNESCO World Heritage setting
* Unique ceremony options: gardens, poolside pergola, or open grain fields
* Taverna di Mozart restaurant onsite
* Outdoor spaces for up to 200 guests
* Courtyard, pergola, and private gardens
* Antique furnishings and wooden beams

## Cons

* Only 9 rooms (24 guests onsite) — very limited accommodation
* Remote Val d'Orcia location — far from Florence
* Less tree cover than beauty-first estates
* Restaurant seats only 60 indoors

## Verdict

The pool and Val d'Orcia setting are genuinely special, but the limited accommodation and remote location are practical challenges for a larger wedding. Moved down under the new brief because tree cover and estate beauty are now prioritized over pool.
