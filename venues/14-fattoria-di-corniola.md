## Location

Empoli, Metropolitan City of Florence, Tuscany. Under 1 hour from Florence airport.

## Why it ranks here

An organic farm estate with a restored historic villa in the Tuscan hills, producing Chianti wine and olive oil. More agriturismo than grand venue.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€35k–€55k**

## Pros

* Historic restored villa with scenic hilltop park
* Organic farm with vineyards and olive groves — Chianti wine production
* Two floors of reception halls with large windows
* Farm-to-table Tuscan cuisine
* Good proximity to Florence, Siena, and Pisa
* Events for 40–150 guests
* Affordable option

## Cons

* Very limited accommodation — only 8 rooms (16 guests onsite)
* No pool confirmed
* Less visually dramatic than top-ranked venues
* More rustic agriturismo feel than luxury estate
* Most guests need offsite lodging

## Verdict

Budget-friendly organic farm option with Chianti character. Best if you want an authentic farm-to-table Tuscan experience and can handle the accommodation logistics.
