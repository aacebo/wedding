## Location

Torrita di Siena, Province of Siena, Tuscany. Perched on a ridge in the Val d'Orcia, between Montepulciano and Pienza.

## Why it ranks here

A restored 17th-century farmhouse with breathtaking Val d'Orcia panoramas. Moves up under the new brief because of its outstanding natural beauty and intimate atmosphere, even though it's more remote.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€55k–€85k** (exclusive-use, 3-night minimum)

## Pros

* Breathtaking Val d'Orcia panoramic views from the ridge
* Pool onsite
* 5 restored historic farmhouse buildings with character
* Exclusive-use only — full privacy guaranteed
* Estate gardens, stone pizza oven, bespoke Tuscan menus from estate ingredients
* Intimate, boutique feel

## Cons

* Only 12 rooms/suites (24–36 guests) — very limited accommodation
* Remote location in Val d'Orcia — far from Florence
* 3-night minimum stay required
* Better suited for smaller, intimate celebrations than 100-guest weddings

## Verdict

Outstanding beauty and intimacy, but the small capacity and remote location make it challenging for a 100-guest event. Best if you're open to a smaller guest list or can arrange significant offsite accommodation.
