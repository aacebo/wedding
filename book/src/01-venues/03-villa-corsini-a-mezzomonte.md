> **Official site:** [villacorsini.com](https://www.villacorsini.com) · **Instagram:** [@villa_corsini](https://www.instagram.com/villa_corsini/)

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Impruneta — 7 km from Florence, overlooking Chianti
> - **Budget:** €70k–€110k for ~100 guests (venue hire ~€12k–€12.5k + VAT for Saturday)
> - **Sleeps:** ~44 guests across estate villas
> - **Pool:** Yes — on the estate
> - **Capacity:** Large events in both indoor halls and garden spaces
> - **Vibe:** Renaissance grandeur, museum-quality interiors

## ✅ The Case For

The interiors are genuinely extraordinary. The Galleria was frescoed in 1631 by Pandolfo Sacchi and Passignano, and the rest of the villa was painted by some of the finest artists working in 17th-century Florence — Giovanni da San Giovanni, Cecco Bravo, and others. You're not renting an event space; you're having dinner surrounded by museum-quality frescoes.

Outside, formal gardens with cypress avenues, rose gardens, and boxwood hedges lead into a shaded English Garden with ancient trees — perfect for ceremonies and aperitifs. The "garden of Meridione" is specifically designed for outdoor events. Both Italian and English garden areas are available, giving you options for different parts of the day.

At just 7 km from Florence, this is the closest to the city of any venue on the list besides Villa Le Fontanelle. Guests can stay in central Florence and cab over in 15 minutes.

> [!NOTE]
> **Historical Significance**
>
> The interior 17th-century frescoes were painted by some of the most important artists of Baroque Florence. The Galleria alone is a reason to visit Impruneta.

## 💀 The Case Against

This is likely the most expensive option on the list, and it should be — you're paying for proximity to Florence and genuinely historic interiors. The 44-guest accommodation covers less than half your guest list, so you'll need to arrange overflow housing (though Florence is right there).

> [!WARNING]
> **Accommodation Gap**
>
> 44 guests onsite out of 100. The rest need hotels — but Florence is 7 km away, so this is manageable with shuttles or taxis.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: maximum historic grandeur**
>
> If you want your guests to feel like they've stepped into a Renaissance painting, this is the venue. The frescoed halls are unmatched on this list. Accept the premium pricing and solve accommodation with nearby Florence.
