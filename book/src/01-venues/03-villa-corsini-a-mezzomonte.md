## Location

Impruneta, 7 km from Florence, overlooking the Chianti hills. Province of Florence, Tuscany.

## Why it ranks here

One of the strongest aesthetic options overall. A serious upgrade under the new brief because beauty and tree-filled grounds matter more than pool emphasis.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€70k–€110k** (venue hire alone ~€12,000–€12,500 + VAT for Saturday high season)

## Pros

* Major grandeur and historic atmosphere — 900+ sqm of frescoed interior halls
* Famous Gallery frescoed in 1631 by Pandolfo Sacchi and Passignano
* Rooms include Sala del Gordigiani, Salotti di Meridione, Sala Primavera, and Salone dei Putti
* Formal gardens with cypress avenues, rose gardens, boxwoods, and a shaded English Garden with ancient trees
* Very strong ceremony and reception backdrop — both Italian and English gardens available
* One of the most visually impressive venues here
* Onsite accommodation for up to ~44 guests across villas on the estate
* Pool available on the estate

## Cons

* Likely the most expensive option on this list
* Accommodation (44 guests) won't cover all 100 — overflow logistics needed

## Verdict

Choose this if you want maximum visual impact and historic grandeur. Accommodation and pool are better than originally thought, though overflow housing will still be needed.
