# Villa Grabau

> **Official site:** [villagrabau.it](https://www.villagrabau.it)

A formal villa near Lucca with frescoed salons, period furniture from India and China, and a 9-hectare botanical park that feels like it belongs in a novel. Three swimming pools. An open-air theatre made of hedges. This place delivers.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** San Pancrazio, 7 km from Lucca — ~40 min from Pisa Airport, ~1.5 hrs from Florence
> - **Budget:** €65k–€95k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 56+ guests (four rental houses, a B&B, plus L'Arancera with 12 bedrooms)
> - **Pool:** Yes — 3 pools on the estate, plus an 18m infinity pool at L'Arancera
> - **Capacity:** 300+ guests
> - **Vibe:** Botanical grandeur, old-world elegance

## ✅ The Case For

The park is the star. Nine hectares of botanical gardens with an English Garden, an Italian Garden, a 17th-century Limonaia filled with ancient citrus trees, and the Teatro di Verzura — an open-air theatre carved from living hedges. For a wedding that wants to feel immersed in nature without sacrificing sophistication, this is hard to beat.

Inside the villa, the rooms are furnished with period pieces including Indian and Chinese furniture and painted ceilings. The ground floor is fully appointed for entertaining — these are lived-in spaces with character, not stripped-back event halls.

The practical side is surprisingly strong too. Four independent houses across the estate give you real accommodation options, and L'Arancera (the sister property) alone sleeps 24–32 guests in 12 bedrooms with its own 18m infinity pool. Between all the properties, you can house most of a 100-person guest list onsite.

> [!TIP]
> **The Trees**
>
> If trees and shade are your top priority, Villa Grabau is probably unbeatable on this list. The 9-hectare botanical park is a genuine garden of international note, not just "nice grounds."

## 💀 The Case Against

It's near Lucca, not Florence — about 90 minutes by car. That's fine if guests are flying into Pisa (40 min away), but it's a real distance if Florence is the anchor city. Pricing is also less transparent than some venues, so expect negotiation.

> [!WARNING]
> **Location**
>
> 1.5 hours from Florence. If proximity to Florence matters, this drops. If Pisa airport works, it's actually convenient.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: beauty + trees + practicality**
>
> The best all-rounder on the list. Extraordinary grounds, strong accommodation, multiple pools, and a villa interior that matches the gardens. The only real trade-off is the Lucca location.
