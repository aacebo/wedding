# Lupaia

> **Official site:** [lupaia.com](https://www.lupaia.com)

A restored 17th-century farmhouse perched on a ridge in the Val d'Orcia, with panoramic views that stretch from Montepulciano to Pienza. If you scaled the guest list down and wanted the most purely beautiful setting possible, this might be the one.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Torrita di Siena, Val d'Orcia — between Montepulciano and Pienza
> - **Budget:** €55k–€85k for ~100 guests (exclusive-use, 3-night minimum)
> - **Sleeps:** 24–36 guests (12 rooms/suites across 5 buildings)
> - **Pool:** Yes
> - **Capacity:** Limited — designed for intimate gatherings
> - **Vibe:** Boutique farmhouse, Val d'Orcia panoramas

## ✅ The Case For

The Val d'Orcia is a UNESCO World Heritage landscape, and Lupaia sits right on top of it. The views from the ridge are the kind of thing that makes people put down their phones and just look. Five restored farmhouse buildings are scattered across the property, each with its own character, and the whole estate operates on an exclusive-use basis — you rent the entire place.

The food is bespoke Tuscan cooking from estate ingredients, there's a stone pizza oven for casual nights, and the intimacy of the property means the wedding feels like a private house party rather than an event at a venue.

> [!TIP]
> **The Views**
>
> The Val d'Orcia panorama from this ridge is genuinely one of the most beautiful landscapes in Italy. UNESCO agrees.

## 💀 The Case Against

The capacity is the hard limit. Twelve rooms across five buildings sleep 24–36 guests, and the property is designed for intimate gatherings, not 100-person weddings. The 3-night minimum and remote Val d'Orcia location add both cost and complexity. This works for a smaller wedding or requires significant offsite logistics.

> [!WARNING]
> **Too Small for 100 Guests**
>
> With 24–36 guests onsite and no large-event infrastructure, Lupaia is realistically suited for a much smaller celebration. A 100-guest wedding here would require major offsite arrangements in a remote area.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: intimate beauty in the Val d'Orcia**
>
> If you scaled the guest list to 30–40, this might jump to #1. At 100 guests, it's a logistical stretch — but the beauty is undeniable.
