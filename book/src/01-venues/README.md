> [!NOTE]
> **Why the rankings shifted**
>
> The original list weighted pool access heavily — great for a swim-weekend vibe, but that's not what this wedding needs. The re-ranking pushes pure estate beauty and tree-filled grounds to the top. That's why **Villa di Maiano**, **Villa Grabau**, and **Villa Corsini** all jumped up, while **Locanda in Tuscany** (previously ranked on pool strength alone) dropped.

> [!TIP]
> **Top 3 at a Glance**
