## Location

Nocera Umbra, Province of Perugia, Umbria. 800m hilltop near the Tuscany/Marche border.

## Why it ranks here

A restored 13th-century hilltop borgo on 50 private acres with olive groves and sweeping mountain views. Exclusive-use estate with a private chapel.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€40k–€65k**

## Pros

* 13th-century castle/borgo, beautifully restored
* Private chapel for ceremonies
* Pool onsite
* 50 acres with olive groves and panoramic mountain views
* 17 suites (51 guests onsite) + nearby hotel adds to 75–80 total
* Multiple courtyards and terraces
* On-site herb and vegetable gardens, farm-to-table dining
* Exclusive-use estate

## Cons

* Remote Umbrian location — far from Florence
* Hilltop access may be challenging for some guests
* Less grand than top-tier Tuscan villas
* Accommodation still falls short of 100 guests

## Verdict

A charming hilltop borgo with a private chapel and good accommodation. Best for couples drawn to the medieval hamlet experience who don't need Florence proximity.
