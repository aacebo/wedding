# Villa Le Fontanelle

> **Official site:** [villalefontanelle.com](https://www.villalefontanelle.com)

A Medici-built residence turned 4-star boutique hotel, minutes from central Florence. It has its own restaurant, a pool, and the kind of refined intimacy that larger estates can't replicate. This is the venue for people who want Florence at their doorstep.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Just outside Florence — one of the closest venues to the city
> - **Budget:** €60k–€90k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 25 guests (7 suites, 14 standard / 25 with extra beds)
> - **Pool:** Yes — onsite
> - **Capacity:** 150 outdoors, 60 indoors
> - **Vibe:** Intimate boutique hotel, Florentine elegance

## ✅ The Case For

The 4-star hotel status is a genuine differentiator. This isn't just a villa you rent — it's a professionally run boutique hotel with a full-service restaurant (Citrarium), a proper concierge operation, and suites that are designed for guests, not repurposed bedrooms. That takes a huge amount of wedding logistics off your plate.

The setting is elegant without being overwhelming. Gardens, a pool, and the kind of curated intimacy where everything feels considered. For a wedding that's more about warmth and refinement than spectacle and scale, the tone here is exactly right.

And the Florence proximity is unmatched. Guests who want to spend a morning at the Uffizi or an afternoon shopping on the Ponte Vecchio can do that and still be back at the venue for aperitivo.

> [!NOTE]
> **It's a Hotel**
>
> Villa Le Fontanelle is a registered 4-star boutique hotel with its own restaurant — not just a private villa. That means professional hospitality infrastructure, not DIY event management.

## 💀 The Case Against

At 25 guests max, the accommodation is small. It's a boutique hotel, not an estate — the scale is intimate by design. If your vision is a sprawling, multi-day country house weekend, this will feel too compact. And it's less visually dramatic than the grand estates at the top of the list.

> [!WARNING]
> **Intimate Scale**
>
> 25 guests onsite, and the overall feel is boutique rather than grand. This works beautifully for controlled elegance, but not for a sprawling Tuscan house party.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: Florence access + controlled luxury**
>
> The right choice if you want your wedding venue to run like a hotel, with Florence minutes away and a restaurant handling the food. Intimate, polished, and logistically simple.
