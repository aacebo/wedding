## Location

Just outside Florence, Medici-built residence. Province of Florence, Tuscany.

## Why it ranks here

Best intimate Florence-area option. Still strong under the new brief because it blends elegance, greenery, and a usable pool. Notably, this is a registered **4-star boutique hotel** with its own restaurant (Citrarium), not just a private villa — a meaningful distinction for guest experience.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€60k–€90k**

## Pros

* Refined and intimate 4-star boutique hotel experience
* Near Florence — one of the closest options to the city
* Pool onsite
* Strong garden setting and good atmosphere
* Onsite restaurant (Citrarium) simplifies catering logistics
* 7 suites sleeping 14 guests standard, up to 25 with extra beds
* Capacity for up to 150 guests outdoors, 60 indoors

## Cons

* Limited rooms (25 guests max onsite) — most guests need offsite accommodation
* Less grand than the very top beauty-first estates
* Better for a smaller-feeling luxury wedding than a large sprawling one

## Verdict

Best fit if you want intimacy, Florence access, and controlled luxury. The 4-star hotel status and onsite restaurant are practical advantages over purely private villas.
