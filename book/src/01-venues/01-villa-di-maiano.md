## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Fiesole hills — 5 km from Florence, 15 min from the Duomo
> - **Budget:** €60k–€95k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 70 guests onsite (19 rooms in the medieval borgo + Farmhouse apartments)
> - **Pool:** Yes — at the Farmhouse complex, with panoramic views
> - **Capacity:** Up to 500 outdoors
> - **Vibe:** Grand Tuscan villa, cinematic beauty

## ✅ The Case For

This is the closest thing on the list to the Villa di Striano visual brief. The villa sits on a ridge above Florence with a panoramic terrace that looks out over the entire city. The grounds are mature and green — old trees, a formal garden with a well and gazebo, and a neo-Gothic loggia beside a rectangular pool.

Inside, the rooms have their original furniture. The Tapestry Room is the showpiece — a monumental reception hall with a sandstone balustrade — and the Red Lounge is the actual room where Ivory shot scenes for *A Room with a View*. These aren't event spaces dressed up to look historic; they're genuinely historic spaces that happen to work beautifully for events.

Accommodation is stronger than you'd expect. The medieval borgo has 19 rooms, and the Farmhouse complex adds apartments — together they sleep around 70, which covers most of a 100-person guest list without anyone needing a hotel.

> [!TIP]
> **The Film Connection**
>
> James Ivory chose this villa for *A Room with a View* because it looked exactly like what it is — an untouched 15th-century Florentine estate. That visual authenticity is still there.

## 💀 The Case Against

This is a beauty-first venue, not an operationally simple one. The prestige of the Fiesole location comes with Florence-area pricing, and the villa's character means you're working around historic spaces rather than purpose-built event infrastructure.

> [!WARNING]
> **Premium Pricing**
>
> Fiesole/Florence commands top rates. This will be at the upper end of the budget range, and the beauty premium is real — you're paying for the setting, not for operational convenience.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: pure visual impact**
>
> If the venue needs to be the most beautiful place your guests have ever been, this is the one. Pool confirmed, accommodation for 70, and a setting that literally attracted a film crew looking for the most romantic villa in Tuscany.
