## Location

Fiesole hills, 5 km from Florence (~15 min from the Duomo). Via del Salviatino 1, Fiesole, Firenze.

## Why it ranks here

Best pure beauty fit under the new brief. One of the strongest estates in the whole list if visual impact matters more than swim utility. A 15th-century villa famously used as a filming location for *A Room with a View*.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€60k–€95k**

## Pros

* Extremely strong beauty score
* Mature grounds and greenery with panoramic terrace overlooking Florence
* Feels closest to the Striano-level visual brief
* Excellent ceremony/photo setting
* Strong onsite accommodation — up to 70 guests across 19 rooms in the medieval borgo plus apartments in the Farmhouse complex
* Pool confirmed onsite at the Farmhouse with panoramic views
* Capacity up to 500 guests outdoors

## Cons

* More beauty-first than operationally easy
* Premium pricing for the Florence/Fiesole location

## Verdict

Best choice if beauty is the main thing. Pool is confirmed onsite, and accommodation is stronger than initially expected.
