## Location

Montespertoli, heart of Chianti, ~35 minutes from Florence. Province of Florence, Tuscany.

## Why it ranks here

Still elite. It loses a bit of edge only because the old ranking overweighted pool. It remains one of the best balanced choices in the whole set. A beautiful 19th-century farmhouse surrounded by 40 hectares of vineyards and olive groves with organic wine production.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€65k–€95k** (from ~£39,500/€46k for 50 guests/2-night stay, scaling up for 100)

## Pros

* Beautiful estate with gardens, vines, and olive groves across 40 hectares
* Infinity pool with stunning views overlooking the Chianti landscape — clearly works for the jump-in requirement
* Notable outdoor spaces: Piazza of the Sphinxes, Belvedere Terrace, and luxurious Mediterranean/tropical gardens
* Strong overall destination feel
* Capacity for 130–180 guests for the event
* Good wedding practicality

## Cons

* Limited onsite rooms — sleeps only 18 guests (8 suites + Honeymoon Nest) at ~€230/guest/night
* Expensive relative to sleeping capacity — significant overflow accommodation needed
* Less formal-villa feeling than Maiano, Grabau, or Corsini

## Verdict

Safest balanced option if you want beauty but do not want pool uncertainty. The infinity pool is a standout feature, but be prepared to arrange offsite accommodation for most guests.
