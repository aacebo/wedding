> **Official site:** [tenutacorbinaia.com](https://www.tenutacorbinaia.com)

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Montespertoli, Chianti — ~35 min from Florence
> - **Budget:** €65k–€95k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 18 guests onsite (8 suites + Honeymoon Nest, ~€230/night)
> - **Pool:** Yes — infinity pool with panoramic Chianti views
> - **Capacity:** 130–180 guests
> - **Vibe:** Chianti wine estate, relaxed luxury

## ✅ The Case For

The infinity pool is the headline. It sits at the edge of the property with an unbroken view across the Chianti landscape — sky meeting earth, vineyards rolling to the horizon. For a post-ceremony jump-in, this is as good as it gets on the list. No ambiguity, no "we should confirm" — this pool was made for the moment.

Beyond the pool, the estate has serious character. Forty hectares of organic vineyards and olive groves, a Piazza of the Sphinxes, a Belvedere Terrace for sunset cocktails, and Mediterranean gardens that blend into tropical plantings.

The estate produces its own organic wine, which adds a Chianti authenticity that's hard to manufacture. This isn't a villa pretending to be a farm — it's a working agricultural estate that also happens to be gorgeous.

> [!TIP]
> **The Pool**
>
> If the post-ceremony jump-in is the single most important moment of the day, Tenuta Corbinaia is the safest choice on the list. The infinity pool is dramatic, confirmed, and purpose-built for exactly this kind of moment.

## 💀 The Case Against

The accommodation is the clear weakness. Eight suites and a Honeymoon Nest sleep just 18 guests — meaning 80+ people need offsite lodging. At €230 per guest per night, the onsite rooms aren't cheap either. The farmhouse aesthetic, while charming, is less formally impressive than Maiano, Grabau, or Corsini.

> [!WARNING]
> **Accommodation Crunch**
>
> Only 18 guests can sleep onsite. For a 100-person wedding, you'll need to arrange hotels or agriturismos in the surrounding Chianti area for the vast majority of your guest list.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: guaranteed pool + Chianti beauty**
>
> The safest balanced option if the jump-in is non-negotiable and you want a genuine Chianti wine estate setting. Accept the accommodation logistics and enjoy the infinity pool.
