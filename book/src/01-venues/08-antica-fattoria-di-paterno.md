## Location

Montespertoli, Province of Florence, Tuscany. Chianti region, 35 minutes from Florence.

## Why it ranks here

A charming 17th-century country villa in the Chianti hills surrounded by vineyards, olive groves, and woods. Strong practical option with good accommodation capacity and multiple elegant reception spaces.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€50k–€80k**

## Pros

* 17th-century tower with rooftop terrace
* Multiple frescoed halls: Hall of Roses, Hall of Mirrors, Hall of the Fireplace, Hall of Queen Helen
* Italian adorned garden, loggia, and antique wine cellar
* Pool onsite
* Strong accommodation — 70 guests total (14 in main tower + 56 in farmhouse apartments)
* Events up to 200 guests
* Chianti vineyard setting, 35 min from Florence

## Cons

* Less visually dramatic than the top-tier beauty estates
* More rustic farmhouse feel than grand villa
* Chianti location means guests need transport

## Verdict

A solid practical choice with good capacity, multiple character spaces, and Chianti charm. Not the most visually stunning, but reliable and well-equipped for larger weddings.
