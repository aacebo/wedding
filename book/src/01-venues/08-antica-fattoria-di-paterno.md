# Antica Fattoria di Paterno

> **Official site:** [anticafattoriadipaterno.com](https://www.anticafattoriadipaterno.com)

A 17th-century Chianti farmhouse with a tower, frescoed halls with names like "Hall of Mirrors" and "Hall of Queen Helen," and enough onsite beds for 70 guests. The most practical large-wedding option in the mid-range.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Montespertoli, Chianti — 35 min from Florence
> - **Budget:** €50k–€80k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 70 guests (14 in tower + 56 in farmhouse apartments)
> - **Pool:** Yes
> - **Capacity:** Up to 200 guests
> - **Vibe:** Chianti farmhouse, charming and capable

## ✅ The Case For

This is the workhorse venue. Seventy guests can sleep onsite across the main tower and surrounding farmhouse apartments — that's the second-best accommodation on the entire list after Castello di Petrata. Events scale up to 200 guests without feeling stretched, and there are multiple distinct reception spaces: the Hall of Roses, the Hall of Mirrors, the Hall of the Fireplace, and the Hall of Queen Helen, plus a loggia, an antique wine cellar, and an Italian adorned garden.

The tower itself has a rooftop terrace, which is a unique ceremony or cocktail hour option. And at 35 minutes from Florence, the location is convenient without paying Florence prices.

> [!TIP]
> **Accommodation Champion**
>
> 70 guests onsite — the best accommodation-to-price ratio on the list. Most of your guest list can sleep where they celebrate.

## 💀 The Case Against

It's a farmhouse, not a villa. The aesthetic is rustic Chianti charm rather than grand Florentine elegance, and the visual impact doesn't compete with the top-tier beauty estates. It's a venue that does many things well rather than one thing brilliantly.

> [!WARNING]
> **Rustic, Not Grand**
>
> If your priority is a venue that makes people gasp, this isn't it. It's the venue that makes people comfortable — which is a different kind of value.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: large weddings that need accommodation**
>
> The right pick if sleeping 70 guests onsite and handling 200 for the party matters more than having the most beautiful venue on the list. Reliable, capable, and fairly priced.
