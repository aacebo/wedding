# Locanda in Tuscany

> **Official site:** [locandaintuscany.it](https://www.locandaintuscany.it)

A restored 17th-century mansion in the UNESCO Val d'Orcia with a 20-metre pool overlooking golden rolling fields. The original pool-first pick — and if you could have your ceremony in the middle of a grain field at sunset, this is the place that actually offers that.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Castiglione d'Orcia, Val d'Orcia — UNESCO World Heritage site
> - **Budget:** €50k–€80k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 24 guests (9 rooms)
> - **Pool:** Yes — 20m pool with valley panorama
> - **Capacity:** 200 outdoors, 60 in restaurant
> - **Vibe:** Tuscan countryside romance, golden fields

## ✅ The Case For

The 20-metre pool with Val d'Orcia views is one of the best pool setups on the entire list — if the post-ceremony jump matters, this pool delivers a genuinely cinematic moment. But the real magic here is the landscape. The UNESCO-listed Val d'Orcia is the Tuscany of postcards: rolling golden fields, lone cypress trees, soft light.

Locanda offers a ceremony option that nobody else on this list can match: a proper sit-down dinner set up in the middle of an open grain field at sunset. It's theatrical in the best way. The onsite Taverna di Mozart handles the food, and the courtyard, pergola, and private gardens give you multiple settings throughout the day.

> [!TIP]
> **The Grain Field Dinner**
>
> A dinner table set in the middle of a golden field at sunset — this is the most photogenic single moment any venue on this list can offer.

## 💀 The Case Against

This venue dropped from its original ranking because the new brief de-prioritizes pool-centric appeal. The property has less tree cover and shade than the top-ranked estates, and the accommodation is thin — 9 rooms for 24 guests in a remote location where offsite options are also limited.

> [!WARNING]
> **Remote + Limited Accommodation**
>
> 24 guests onsite in a remote Val d'Orcia location. Finding overflow accommodation is harder here than near Florence or Lucca — there simply aren't as many options nearby.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: cinematic pool moments + field dinners**
>
> If you're drawn to the image of a golden-field dinner and a pool with endless views, this is the one. Just be honest about whether the remote location and limited accommodation work for your guest list.
