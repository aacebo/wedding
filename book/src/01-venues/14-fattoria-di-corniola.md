# Fattoria di Corniola

> **Official site:** [fattoriadicorniola.com](https://www.fattoriadicorniola.com) · **Instagram:** [@fattoriadicorniola](https://www.instagram.com/fattoriadicorniola/)

An organic farm estate near Empoli that produces its own Chianti wine and olive oil. This is the most authentically agricultural venue on the list — a working farm that happens to have a beautiful hilltop villa and reception halls with big windows overlooking the vines.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Empoli — under 1 hour from Florence airport
> - **Budget:** €35k–€55k for ~100 guests
> - **Sleeps:** 16 guests (8 rooms — 6 in villa, 2 in park apartment)
> - **Pool:** Not confirmed — verify directly with venue
> - **Capacity:** 40–150 guests
> - **Vibe:** Working organic farm, rustic Tuscan authenticity

## ✅ The Case For

The food story is the draw here. This is a genuine organic farm producing Chianti wine and olive oil, and the kitchen cooks farm-to-table Tuscan cuisine from what's growing on the property. Two floors of reception halls with large windows look out over the vineyards, and the hilltop park gives you a scenic outdoor ceremony option.

At €35k–€55k, this is the most affordable venue on the entire list. If budget is a real constraint and you want Tuscan character rather than Tuscan grandeur, this delivers.

> [!TIP]
> **Most Affordable**
>
> €35k–€55k is the lowest price point on the list. If you want Tuscany without the Tuscany markup, this is it.

## 💀 The Case Against

The accommodation barely exists — 8 rooms for 16 guests means nearly everyone needs offsite lodging. No pool has been confirmed, which is a significant gap under the current brief. And the overall aesthetic is honest agriturismo, not polished luxury — which is either exactly what you want or exactly what you don't.

> [!WARNING]
> **Very Limited Accommodation + No Confirmed Pool**
>
> 16 guests onsite and no verified pool. For a 100-guest wedding where the pool jump matters, this creates two significant logistical problems.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **To Confirm**
>
> Does the property have a pool? The official site and directories don't clearly show one — contact the venue directly to verify.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: authentic farm experience on a budget**
>
> The right choice if farm-to-table authenticity and price matter more than luxury amenities. Understand the accommodation and pool limitations before committing.
