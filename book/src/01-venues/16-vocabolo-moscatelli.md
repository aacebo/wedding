# Vocabolo Moscatelli

> **Official site:** [vocabolomoscatelli.com](https://www.vocabolomoscatelli.com)

An 800-year-old monastery reimagined as a boutique design hotel, wrapped in ancient woodland in an Umbrian valley. Bamboo domes, a travertine pool, a 30-metre pergola, and a private chapel. It's the most architecturally distinctive venue on this list — and the least practical for a 100-guest wedding.

## 📋 At a Glance

> [!NOTE]
> **Quick Facts**
>
> - **Location:** Calzolaro (near Umbertide), Umbria — quiet valley in ancient woodland
> - **Budget:** €50k–€80k for ~100 guests (buyout ~€18,000/night in high season)
> - **Sleeps:** 24 guests (12 rooms)
> - **Pool:** Yes — travertine pool
> - **Capacity:** ~50 guests maximum
> - **Vibe:** Design-forward monastery, intimate and architectural

## ✅ The Case For

This is the venue for someone who cares about design as much as location. The travertine pool, the bamboo domes, the fire pit garden, the 30-metre pergola, the restaurant winter garden — every detail has been considered with a designer's eye. The private chapel handles ceremonies, the Bar Matite handles cocktails, and the bridal and spa suites are genuinely luxurious.

The ancient woodland setting gives it a quiet, otherworldly atmosphere that none of the louder, more event-oriented venues can match. If your vision is a small, impossibly beautiful gathering in a place that feels discovered rather than booked, Vocabolo Moscatelli delivers that.

> [!NOTE]
> **Design Distinction**
>
> Bamboo domes, travertine pool, 30m pergola, fire pit garden — this is the only venue on the list where architecture and design are the primary draw rather than history or landscape.

## 💀 The Case Against

The 50-guest cap makes this fundamentally incompatible with a 100-guest wedding. Even if you could expand capacity, 12 rooms for 24 guests means most people are offsite in a remote Umbrian valley. The buyout at €18,000 per night is steep for what is essentially a 50-person venue.

> [!WARNING]
> **50-Guest Maximum**
>
> This venue cannot host 100 guests. Period. If you're committed to that guest count, Vocabolo Moscatelli is off the table regardless of how beautiful it is.

## 🏆 Verdict

> [!TIP]
> **Best for: a smaller, design-obsessed celebration**
>
> Stunningly designed and architecturally unique, but only works if you're willing to cut the guest list in half. At 40–50 guests, this jumps significantly up the rankings. At 100, it's not viable.
