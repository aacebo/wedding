## Location

Calzolaro (near Umbertide), Province of Perugia, Umbria. An 800-year-old monastery in a quiet Umbrian valley wrapped in ancient woodland.

## Why it ranks here

A beautifully designed boutique property with a private chapel and distinctive design touches (bamboo domes, travertine pool, 30m pergola). Lowest ranked because of its very small capacity and remote location.

## Estimated all-in budget for ~100 guests

* **€50k–€80k** (buyout ~€18,000/night in high season)

## Pros

* Private chapel for ceremonies
* Travertine pool
* 30m pergola and event garden
* Boutique design hotel with distinctive character
* Bar Matite, bridal suite, spa suite
* Bamboo domes and fire pit garden
* Restaurant winter garden
* Ancient woodland setting

## Cons

* Only 12 rooms (24 guests) — most guests offsite
* Events capped at ~50 guests — too small for a 100-guest wedding without expansion
* Very expensive buyout (€18k/night)
* Remote Umbrian location
* Least practical for the stated 100-guest brief

## Verdict

A stunningly designed boutique property, but the 50-guest event cap and 24-guest accommodation make it impractical for a 100-guest wedding. Only viable if you significantly reduce the guest count or arrange extensive external logistics.
