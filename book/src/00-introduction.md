# Baicebo Wedding

Picture this: late afternoon sun catching the stone walls of an old Tuscan villa, centuries-old trees throwing dappled shade across the lawn, your closest people gathered in a place so beautiful it barely needs decoration — and then, at the end of the ceremony, a joyful, fully-clothed jump into the pool.

That's the vision. This book is the research that gets us there.

We started with sixteen venues across Tuscany and Umbria and scored them against what actually matters to us. The list has been re-ranked, fact-checked, and whittled down to a clear shortlist.

> [!NOTE]
> **What We're Looking For**
>
> 1. **Beauty and atmosphere** — the venue should take your breath away. Think Villa di Striano-level visual impact.
> 2. **Trees and shaded grounds** — mature gardens, canopy cover, dappled light. Not a sun-baked terrace.
> 3. **A pool for the post-ceremony jump** — doesn't need to be a resort pool, just needs to exist and be jump-in-able.
> 4. **Wedding practicality** — can it actually host 100 people for a ceremony, dinner, and party?
> 5. **Pricing and value** — we're spending real money, so it should feel worth it.

---

## 🏆 The Shortlist

### 🌟 Final 5

1. **[Villa di Maiano](./01-venues/01-villa-di-maiano.md)** — a 15th-century Fiesole estate where they filmed *A Room with a View*
2. **[Villa Grabau](./01-venues/02-villa-grabau.md)** — a 9-hectare botanical park with frescoed salons near Lucca
3. **[Villa Corsini a Mezzomonte](./01-venues/03-villa-corsini-a-mezzomonte.md)** — 900 sqm of frescoed halls overlooking the Chianti hills
4. **[Tenuta Corbinaia](./01-venues/04-tenuta-corbinaia.md)** — a Chianti farmhouse with an infinity pool and 40 hectares of vines
5. **[Villa Le Fontanelle](./01-venues/05-villa-le-fontanelle.md)** — a Medici-built boutique hotel minutes from Florence

> [!TIP]
> **The Final Three**
>
> 1. **Villa di Maiano** — if beauty is everything, this is the one. Panoramic Florence views, medieval borgo, confirmed pool.
> 2. **Villa Grabau** — the best mix of beauty, trees, and practicality. Three pools, strong accommodation, massive botanical park.
> 3. **Tenuta Corbinaia** — the safest bet. Stunning infinity pool, organic wine estate, no ambiguity about the jump-in.

> [!WARNING]
> **Pool Check**
>
> If the post-ceremony jump is non-negotiable, **Tenuta Corbinaia**, **Villa Le Fontanelle**, and **Villa Scorzi** have clearly confirmed, jump-ready pools. At **Villa di Maiano** and **Villa Corsini**, the pool exists but you should confirm the setup directly with the venue before committing.
