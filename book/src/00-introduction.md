# Baicebo Wedding

Picture this: late afternoon sun catching the stone walls of an old Tuscan villa, centuries-old trees throwing dappled shade across the lawn, your closest people gathered in a place so beautiful it barely needs decoration — and then, at the end of the ceremony, a joyful, fully-clothed jump into the pool.

That's the vision. This book is the research that gets us there.

We started with sixteen venues across Tuscany and Umbria and scored them against what actually matters to us. The list has been re-ranked, fact-checked, and whittled down to a clear shortlist.

> [!NOTE]
> **How We Score**
>
> Each venue is rated 1–10 on six attributes, weighted by priority. Weighted score = Σ(rating × weight). Max = 10.0.
>
> | Attribute | Weight | What it means |
> |-----------|--------|---------------|
> | 🏛️ Beauty & Atmosphere | 25% | Visual impact, architecture, grounds, "wow factor" |
> | 🌳 Trees & Shaded Grounds | 20% | Mature gardens, canopy cover, dappled light |
> | 🏊 Pool (jump-in ready) | 15% | Exists, accessible, suitable for the post-ceremony jump |
> | 💒 Wedding Practicality | 15% | Can it host 100 for ceremony, dinner, and party? Accommodation? |
> | 🍸 Nightlife Proximity | 10% | Walking distance or short taxi to bars, restaurants, town life |
> | 💰 Value | 15% | Budget relative to what you get |

## 🏆 The Shortlist

### 🌟 Top 5 by Score

1. **[Villa Grabau](./01-venues/02-villa-grabau.md)** (8.20) — a 9-hectare botanical park with frescoed salons and 3+ pools near Lucca
2. **[Villa di Maiano](./01-venues/01-villa-di-maiano.md)** (8.15) — a 15th-century Fiesole estate where they filmed *A Room with a View*
3. **[Villa Scorzi](./01-venues/06-villa-scorzi.md)** (7.40) — 14th-century villa near Pisa with the best value and a jump-ready infinity pool
4. **[Villa Corsini a Mezzomonte](./01-venues/03-villa-corsini-a-mezzomonte.md)** (7.35) — 900 sqm of frescoed halls overlooking the Chianti hills
5. **[Villa Le Fontanelle](./01-venues/05-villa-le-fontanelle.md)** (7.25) — a Medici-built boutique hotel minutes from Florence nightlife

> [!TIP]
> **The Final Three**
>
> 1. **Villa Grabau** (8.20) — the highest score overall. Best mix of beauty, trees, and practicality. Three pools, strong accommodation, massive botanical park.
> 2. **Villa di Maiano** (8.15) — if beauty is everything, this is the one. Panoramic Florence views, medieval borgo, confirmed pool, and nightlife 15 min away.
> 3. **Villa Scorzi** (7.40) — the best value play. Reliable infinity pool, 36 beds onsite, and the most affordable price point on the list.

> [!WARNING]
> **Pool Check**
>
> If the post-ceremony jump is non-negotiable, **Tenuta Corbinaia**, **Villa Le Fontanelle**, and **Villa Scorzi** have clearly confirmed, jump-ready pools. At **Villa di Maiano** and **Villa Corsini**, the pool exists but you should confirm the setup directly with the venue before committing.
